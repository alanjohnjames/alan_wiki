## Citrix / MacOS Dual Monitors

Citrix, Dual monitor Mac
https://www.reddit.com/r/Citrix/comments/vqzxz4/citrix_dual_monitor_mac/

How to Configure Dual Monitors in Citrix Workspace
https://www.acecloudhosting.com/knowledgebase/configure-dual-monitor-in-citrix-workspace/

Click the “View” button in the menu bar.

![alt text](image.png)

- Choose Your Display Option.
- Click on “Enter Full Screen” if you want full screen only on the primary monitor.
- Click on “Use All Displays In Full Screen” if you want full screen across all connected monitors.
- Drag the Citrix Virtual Desktop window between monitors to extend the display.
